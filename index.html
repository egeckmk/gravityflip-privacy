Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Gravity Flip: Mars ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

Device Information

We may collect certain device information automatically when you use our App, including:

  • Device ID: Your device's advertising identifier or other device-level ID
  • Device Type: Information about your device model, operating system version, and other technical specifications

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our App, including:

  • Product Interaction: App launches, taps, clicks, and other in-app interactions
  • Advertising Data: Information about advertisements you have seen or interacted with
  • Game Data: High scores and game progress (stored locally on your device)

Important: All game data (high scores, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

2. How We Use Your Information

Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. All advertisements shown in our App are non-personalized ads. This means:

  • Your personal data is NOT used to target or personalize advertisements
  • Your browsing history, app usage, or other personal information is NOT used for advertising purposes
  • Advertisements are shown based on general content and context only, not your personal preferences or behavior

Data Linking and User Identity

Data Collection and User Identity: The data we collect (Device ID, Advertising Data) may be linked to your device identity for the purpose of serving non-personalized advertisements and ensuring app functionality. However, this data is NOT used to track you across apps and websites, create user profiles, or show personalized advertisements.

User Tracking

We do NOT track users across apps and websites. Our App does not use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework because we do not engage in user tracking activities. The data we collect is not linked with third-party data for advertising purposes or shared with data brokers for tracking purposes.

Data Usage Purposes

The information we collect is used for:

  • Third-Party Advertising: Displaying non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
  • App Functionality: Ensuring the App works correctly, preventing fraud, and improving user experience
  • Analytics: Understanding how users interact with the App to improve features and fix issues

3. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect limited technical data (device ID, advertising data) to serve non-personalized ads, but:

  • This data is NOT used to create user profiles
  • This data is NOT used to show personalized advertisements
  • This data is NOT used to track users across apps and websites
  • This data is linked to your device identity for app functionality purposes only

For more information about how Google handles your data, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

All game-related data (high scores, settings preferences) is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data:

  • Remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the App
  • Is not accessible by other apps or third parties

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. The limited data collected through AdMob is subject to Google's privacy practices as described above. We do not share your data with data brokers or use it for tracking purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for users of all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review any personal information we may have collected
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of data collection by adjusting your device's advertising settings
  • Uninstall the App at any time, which will remove all locally stored data

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
